How we began

J&P Autos is a family run business that was established by my dad mr Philip Jenkins in 1986, he got the keys to his first garage on sloper road the day i was born! i, Alex Jenkins became a partner in the garage in 2014 and run the business side by side with my dad before taking over fully in 2016.

My Journey

Before i became a loyal and committed garage owner my dad certainly put me through my paces. as a youngster i would often be at the garage on Saturdays and during school holidays as all i wanted to do was learn and i just loved working on cars with my dad. it was during those early years that i developed my passion for anything engine related. I left school at the age of 16 and jumped straight into my mechanical apprenticeship in land rover. here i gained my level 4 NVQ in light vehicle maintenance and repair.

Our Legacy

Along the way i have gained electrical and hybrid training with an exceptional knowledge in electrical diagnostic. since taking over the business i have made it my personal mission to uphold the fantastic reputation my dad worked so hard to establish and i am honoured, as Phils son to carry on his legacy.

What types of services do you provide?

We offer a comprehensive range of vehicle maintenance services, including M.O.T and diagnostic testing, routine servicing (brakes, clutches, batteries, exhausts, and more), as well as selling quality cars at affordable rates.

What are the signs of brake wear?

Common signs include squealing or grinding noises, a soft or spongy brake pedal, vibrations when braking, or an increase in stopping distance. Regular brake checks are crucial to ensure your safety.

Why are scheduled service intervals important?

Scheduled servicing helps maintain your vehicle's performance, improves fuel efficiency, and extends its lifespan. It also ensures early detection of potential issues, saving you from costly repairs.

Can regular maintenance prevent major repairs?

Yes, routine maintenance can identify and address minor issues before they become significant problems. Services like oil changes, filter replacements, and system checks are vital for keeping your car in top condition.
